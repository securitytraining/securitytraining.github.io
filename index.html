About Security force training

Security Force Training is an Elite training academy that will train capable security force personnel and turn them into elite trained professionals who can handle every individual or crowd situation with ease using the techniques and as well earn the respect of attaining a Government Approved certification that will elevate their professional standards among other security personnel.

We salute you!

Security force is the core of today’s society and we salute their hard work but often even though capable ,without a proper training and without proper approved certification they find themselves left behind and don’t earn the proper respect they truly deserve . We aim to train and certify them in the most professional way combining theory and practise of a variety of martial arts and certifying them at the end of the course to be recognised by the society as a group of Elite security forces.

Learn with Fun

We understand that most of the guards are not that comfortable with too much of reading course material , so we have designed this course with minimum instruction and maximum hands on experience so that the course is fun , interactive and the personnel who attend it gain immensely from it both knowledge wise as well as improving their combat skills .It will also help them attain a government approved certificate at the end of the course that would help them gain the confidence and respect they truly deserve in the society .
